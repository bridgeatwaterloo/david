Welcome to David!

David is an interactive contemporary dance production


David is completely at the voyeuristic whims of you and other audience members. He is dependent on those peering into the performance space to tell him what to do next.


Through your mobile phone you can vote, rate, and tap your way through this digitally interactive performance, manipulating the dance, projecting your desires and dictating the sound as you go.
