The David set up

Epoq requires you to prepare your phone properly before building David. Remove auto lock on your phone. Set the sound to silent. Do not browse privately or incognito. Turn on location services.

How it works

In order to build and control David you will work together with the rest of the audience. The majority will rule in some scenes, in others the responsibility will be handed to a single person.

When you see this pulsing image on your screen and hear the slot machine sound it's time to look at your phone. At all other times we encourgage you to look at David and see how your creation is coming to life.
